About Us

At Johnson & Dunn LLC, we empower contractors to succeed by streamlining the often complex processes of claim supplements and job approvals.


With deep industry expertise and a proactive approach, our team specializes in meticulously preparing claim supplements, navigating intricate insurance protocols, and ensuring every detail is in place for job approvals. We understand the challenges contractors face in managing paperwork and regulatory requirements, and we're here to simplify that process for you.


Our commitment to transparency, accuracy, and personalized service means you receive tailored solutions designed to maximize your project's potential. By partnering with John & Dunn LLC, you gain a trusted ally dedicated to protecting your interests and accelerating your success, from claim submission through final job approval.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to help you secure the compensation and authorizations you need, allowing you to focus on delivering quality work without unnecessary administrative burdens.

Our Services

Our services aim to streamline workflows, reduce risks, and most importantly of all, reduce the burden off your shoulders

Insurance Liaisons

Are insurance matters causing you concerns? Johnson & Dunn can assist you with all things related to insurance. We can act as a bridge between you and your insurance company as your insurance liaisons. We are prepared to assist you throughout the claim and appeal processes, and hel p you better understand your insurance needs.

Navigating Protocols

Having a difficult time navigating your insurance policy's complex protocols? No worries, Johnson & Dunn can help. We can aid you in understanding your insurance policy, and clarify the coverage you are entitled to.

Claim Support

Are you preparing to file a claim with your insurance company? Johnson & Dunn can be of assistance throughout this process. We specialize in meticulously preparing claim supplements, to ensure you get the most out of your insurance policy.

Job Estimating

Have a job concerning damage to an exterior? Johnson & Dunn can provide you with a repair estimate. Our inspections are perform by professionals with great experience in the construction field, to ensure you get an accurate estimate of the job.

Clerical Work

Johnson & Dunn's expertise lies in clerical work, ensuring efficient and accurate administrative support for all your operations. We can assist you with insurance matters, and help manage relevant paperwork and regulatory requirements.
