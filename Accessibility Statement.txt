<b>Conformance status</b><br>
<a href="https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG21/">The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG)</a>  defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. It defines the three levels of conformance: level A, Level AA, and level AAA. The Johnson & Dunn Construction Consultation LLC website is conformant with WCAG 2.1 level AA.<br>
